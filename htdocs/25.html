Information

The album 25 is a "make-up record" according to the artist herself. It consists of 11 tracks that are bold, powerful and highly emotional. It shows that she has been through heartbreak and start to feel regret, but eventually found an everlasting love.

One of these tracks is Hello, which is a song about missing her previous relationships and everyone that she loved. In an interview with Opra Whitney back in November 2021, she also mentioned that the song was dedicated for her younger self.

This album also includes one of my personal favorite, Remedy. Adele has found true love in her son Angelo. She explained how she will be the remedy to her son whenever everything just seems unfair.

The next song, Love In The Dark, tells us how Adele's feelings for her partner is fading. She went from heartbroken to heartbreaker. But despite this, she really feels bad for her partner because she knows what it's like to have a heart broken.
